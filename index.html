About

Software engineer driven by curiosity, technical depth, and system understanding.

After more than a decade in the field, I have come to see software engineering as an ongoing exploration rather than a fixed specialization. I learn by digging into source code, understanding underlying layers, and tracing problems back to low level mechanisms, even when that means working in unfamiliar languages or tools.

I approach design with a sense of mechanical sympathy, paying attention to how systems are designed to be used and where their limits lie. I focus more on patterns, constraints, and performance characteristics than on frameworks. Go is the language in which I am most proficient, and I value its ability to stay close to the machine while remaining readable and pragmatic.

Living in Annecy since 2020, I value balance and long term sustainability, both in systems and in teams. I tend to bring a calm presence to collaborative environments and help keep interactions simple and constructive when complexity grows.
